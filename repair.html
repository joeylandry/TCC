Expert Court Repair at Affordable Prices

At Total Court Construction, we go beyond just the surface. Our full suite of pickleball court services is designed to address every aspect of court functionality and aesthetics. Whether you're dealing with structural issues or simply want to refresh your court's appearance, we have the expertise to deliver top-notch results.

What We Offer

Crack Repair and Sealing

02
